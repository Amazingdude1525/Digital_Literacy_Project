# Stay Safe Online: Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

1. **UPI Safety:** Never enter your UPI PIN to *receive* money; a PIN is only required to *send* funds.
2. **UPI Safety:** Do not scan random QR codes sent by strangers online for peer-to-peer marketplace transactions (like buying/selling used books).
3. **Verify the sender's actual email address,** not just their display name, before clicking links in urgent messages.
4. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** on all university, email, and social media accounts.
5. **Avoid logging into banking apps** or making financial transactions while connected to public college Wi-Fi.
6. **Check URLs for "https"** and look for subtle misspellings in web addresses (e.g., "vlt.ac.in" instead of "vit.ac.in") before entering login details.
7. **Regularly update your phone and laptop** operating systems to patch known security vulnerabilities.
8. **Report Incidents:** Immediately report any cyber fraud to the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal (**cybercrime.gov.in**) or call the **1930 Helpline**.
